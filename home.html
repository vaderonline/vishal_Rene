Information About Mahatma Gandhi

Mohandas Karamchand known as Mahatma or "Great-Souled" Gandhi was born in Porbandar, the capital of a small
principality in what is today the state of Gujarat in Western India, where his father was prime minister. His mother was
a profoundly religious Hindu. She and the rest of the Gandhi family belonged to a branch of Hinduism in which
non-violence and tolerance between religious groups were considered very important. His family background has later
been seen as a very important explanation of why Mohandas Gandhi was able to achieve the position he held in Indian society.
